Clubs/teams

What do we have to offer?

At Enver creek we have a wide section of clubs/teams to offer. Here's what we have for our clubs. We have the dance team, ice hockey, weight training, robotics, jazz band, student council, bhangra team, cheese club and lots more. Our students are invited to participate in a variety of athletic activities throughout the school year. Some sports are school-based and some involve meets, games or play days with other Surrey schools at various locations. We appreciate the ongoing support from our coaches, teachers, volunteers.
